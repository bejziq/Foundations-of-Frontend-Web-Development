Mobile application

Avocado Toast is a mobile application that combines social media aspects of photo sharing with a recipe collection and recommendation platform for food enthusiasts.

Target Users: Foodies who enjoy discovering and sharing new dishes. Home cooks looking for inspiration and easy-to-follow recipes. Individuals with dietary restrictions or preferences seeking suitable recipe ideas (e.g., vegan, gluten-free).


Core Functionalities: Photo Sharing: Users can take or upload photos of their food creations and share them with the Avocado Toast community. Recipe Management: The app allows users to create, save, and share recipes with detailed instructions, ingredients lists, and serving sizes. Recipe Discovery: Users can browse a curated feed of food photos and recipes uploaded by other users. Search and Filtering: The app offers search functions to find recipes by ingredients, dish types (e.g., appetizer, main course), cuisine, or dietary restrictions. Social Features: Users can follow other users, like and comment on photos and recipes, and build a community around shared food interests.


Benefits: Food Inspiration: Users can discover new dishes and gain inspiration for their own cooking through the photo sharing and recipe browsing features. Recipe Collection and Organization: The app allows users to save and organize their favorite recipes in a central location for easy access. Dietary Customization: Search and filtering functionalities enable users to find recipes that suit their dietary needs and preferences. Community and Sharing: Avocado Toast fosters a community of food enthusiasts who can share their culinary creations and connect with like-minded individuals.
